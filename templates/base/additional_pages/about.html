About Us

The journey of TeamUp! from idea to reality.

  • 2024

    The Beginning

    It all started with a simple problem - how to quickly and fairly divide players into teams? Instead of manual selection and long discussions, we decided to create a solution that does it in just a few clicks.

  • Development

    Building the Platform

    We developed an intuitive platform that enables team generation for various sports. The focus was on ease of use and fair player distribution using advanced algorithms.

  • Expansion

    Adding More Sports

    After initial success with football, we expanded support to basketball, volleyball, handball, and tennis. Each sport has its own specific formations and distribution rules.

  • Today

    Helping Thousands of Players

    TeamUp! is now used by sports clubs, schools, and groups of friends across the region. Our mission is to simplify sports organization and enable fair play for everyone.
