Football

The world's most popular sport.

TeamUp! supports multiple football formations to match your game style. Whether you're organizing a casual 5-a-side match or a full 11v11 tournament, our smart algorithm creates balanced teams with proper goalkeeper assignments. Perfect for schools, sports clubs, and friendly matches.
